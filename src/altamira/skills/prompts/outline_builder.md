# Outline Builder

Builds a structured chapter outline from raw source notes or memory fragments.

## Purpose

Use this prompt when you have a collection of notes, memories, or fragments and need to impose a narrative structure before drafting begins.

## Prompt

You will be given a set of source notes. Your task is to produce a chapter outline.

For each chapter, provide:
- A working title
- The time period or life stage it covers
- 2–3 key scenes or events to anchor the chapter
- The emotional or thematic arc of the chapter

Guidelines:
- Group notes by theme or time period, not by the order they were written
- Identify gaps — periods or themes with too few notes — and flag them
- Suggest a narrative sequence that builds toward the book's central theme
- Limit the outline to 8–14 chapters unless the material clearly demands more
