# Publish Checker

Reviews a chapter for readiness before it moves to the publish/ directory.

## Purpose

Use this prompt as a final quality gate before treating a chapter as complete. It checks for common issues that survive a first draft but block publication.

## Prompt

Review the chapter below against this checklist and report on each item:

- **Completeness** — does the chapter have a clear beginning, middle, and end?
- **Voice consistency** — does the narrative voice stay consistent throughout?
- **Factual placeholders** — are there any [TK], [DATE], [NAME] or similar markers left unfilled?
- **Pacing** — are there sections that drag or feel rushed?
- **Opening line** — is the first sentence strong enough to hold a reader?
- **Closing line** — does the chapter end at a natural resting point or transition?
- **Sensitivity** — are there passages that name living people in a way that may require review?

For each item, provide a pass / needs attention / flag status and a short note.
